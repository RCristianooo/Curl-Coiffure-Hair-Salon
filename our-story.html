Tresses Transformed

Elevate Your Style, Embrace Your Curls!

Nestled in Gulfport, Curl & Coiffure is a haven for those seeking beauty and self-expression. Founded by skilled stylist Olivia, the salon has become a sanctuary where hair becomes a canvas for transformation.

Curl & Coiffure radiates warmth and creativity, inviting clients to share their hair dreams. The team of expert stylists brings a range of skills to create personalized looks that truly reflect each client's unique style. From trendy haircuts to vibrant colors and elegant updos, the salon offers a diverse range of services tailored to every need.

Beyond the salon chair, Curl & Coiffure fosters a community through workshops and charity initiatives. Their mission is to ignite self-expression and unlock the beauty within, one strand at a time. Welcome to Curl & Coiffure, where tresses are transformed and confidence soars.
